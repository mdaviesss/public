Why should you car-pool?

Cost effective - save your money!


Spare some travel costs of getting to your best weekend of the summer by splitting it with like-minded people coming from or near your area. Book with us today and you could save as much as 80% of what you would to begin with!

Fuel emissions - help the environment


The average vehicle releases 10,000 pounds of carbon dioxide every year. Our aim is to try and combat the current environmental state affected by cars. Car-pooling has proven to help in this, with case studies showing reductions of 35 or 47% in terms of energy consumption and 35 and 65% for CO2Â emissions if used regularly worldwide.

You can help this by playing a small part!

Reduce congestion - get into your festival sooner


Many people experience heavy congestion when getting into festivals and some can be queuing for several hours in traffic jams. This can be addressed simply by sharing cars! By car-pooling, the number of cars going into festivals will be significantly less; if everyone going to festivals did the same, the number of cars on the road could be reduced by 70%, meaning you'd get into your festival sooner and enjoy more of your weekend. It's a no-brainer!
